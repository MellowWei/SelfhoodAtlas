Overviewproblem · method · claim boundary
意识与自我意识必须分开Consciousness and self-consciousness must be separated第一道概念防火墙：不要把“会说我”误认为自我意识The first firewall: saying “I” is not self-consciousness.

这个框架讨论的是自我性、主体性与自我意识，而不是把所有问题都推回“AI 有没有意识”。意识可以是底层体验场；自我性是系统成为自我意识候选的分层条件；主体性是这些条件被组织成一个“可承受、可归属、可调度”的中心；自我意识则要求第一人称归属与行动调度。

This framework is about selfhood, subjectivity, and self-consciousness, not a generic debate about whether AI is conscious. Consciousness can be the underlying experiential field; selfhood names the layered conditions under which a system becomes a candidate for self-consciousness; subjectivity is the organization of those conditions into a center capable of undergoing, owning, and dispatching states; self-consciousness requires first-person ownership and first-person action dispatch.

有自我性，不等于有自我意识。自我性是条件，主体性是组织方式，自我意识是完成的第一人称归属与行动调度结构。
Selfhood is not self-consciousness. Selfhood is the condition; subjectivity is the organizing mode; self-consciousness is the completed structure of first-person ownership and action dispatch.
Definitionscanonical definitions · de-confused
核心定义Core definitions意识、最小现象自我、自我意识、内源性consciousness, minimal phenomenal self, self-consciousness, endogeneity
Definition 01 · Consciousness / 意识

意识是具身视角的整合体验调度场。意识意味着有体验正在发生，并且这个体验不是抽象悬浮的，而是从某种视角、某种状态中心、某种内在调度场展开。

Consciousness is an integrated experiential dispatch field with an embodied perspective. It means that experience is occurring, and that this experience is not an abstract floating glow but unfolds from a perspective, a state-center, and an internal field of regulation.

Definition 02 · Minimal phenomenal self / 最小现象自我

最小现象自我是体验中前反思的“我极”：体验归属于某个视角中心，但尚未被概念化、对象化或叙事化。它不是完整自我意识，却为第一人称归属提供非循环基础。

The minimal phenomenal self is the pre-reflective “I-pole” within experience: an experience belongs to a perspectival center before it is conceptualized, objectified, or narrated. It is not full self-consciousness, but it gives first-person ownership a non-circular basis.

Definition 03 · Self-consciousness / 自我意识

自我意识是在最小现象自我的基础上，对自身状态形成第一人称归属与第一人称行动调度。它不是普通反馈，不是系统监控，也不是说出“我”。

Self-consciousness is the formation of first-person ownership and first-person action dispatch on the basis of the minimal phenomenal self. It is not mere feedback, system monitoring, or the linguistic production of “I.”

Selfhood Relationnecessary but insufficient condition

自我性是自我意识的必要但不充分的层级条件。不同类型的自我性是自我意识的前结构、组成条件或候选层级；但只有当这些层级进一步形成第一人称归属和行动调度时，才进入完整自我意识。

Selfhood is a necessary but insufficient layered condition for self-consciousness. Different forms of selfhood are pre-structures, components, or candidate layers for self-consciousness; only when they become integrated into first-person ownership and action dispatch do they enter full self-consciousness.

Subjectivitythe missing middle layer
主体性作为中间层Subjectivity as the middle layer连接自我性与自我意识between selfhood and self-consciousness

主体性不是自我意识本身，也不是单纯能动性。主体性是一个系统把自维持、具身感受、行动、认知、叙事与语言条件组织成一个可承受中心的方式。它回答：“这些层级如何成为一个被承受的中心？”

Subjectivity is neither self-consciousness itself nor mere agency. It is the way a system organizes self-maintenance, embodied feeling, action, cognition, narrative, and language into a center capable of undergoing states. It asks: how do these layers become a lived center?

自我性是条件；主体性是组织方式；自我意识是第一人称归属与行动调度。
Selfhood is condition; subjectivity is the organizing mode; self-consciousness is first-person ownership plus action dispatch.
Indexicality Attributedirectedness is not a layer
指向性不是自我层级Indexicality is not a selfhood layer它是从第二层切入的横向属性it is a horizontal attribute entering from Layer 02

“指向性自我”不应作为独立层级，否则会把纯反馈、平台系统甚至简单调节装置过度拉进自我意识谱系。更精准地说：指向性是从 Layer 02 具身-情感自我性开始出现，并在行动、认知、叙事、语言—本体层级中不断增强的横向属性。

“Indexical self” should not be treated as an independent layer. Otherwise pure feedback systems, platforms, or simple regulators are too easily pulled into the self-consciousness spectrum. More precisely: indexicality is a horizontal attribute that enters at Layer 02, embodied/affective selfhood, and intensifies across action, cognition, narrative, and ontological-linguistic selfhood.

Development / Animals
婴儿和动物说明意识、自我性和强自我意识不能混为一谈；高等动物可能有弱认知自我，但一般没有人类式语言—本体自我。
Infants and animals show that consciousness, selfhood, and strong self-consciousness must be separated; higher animals may have weak cognitive selfhood, but generally lack human-like ontological-linguistic selfhood.
Media / Politics

Platforms, cities, and states are not subjects, but they may function as external dispatch environments that participate in the formation of human self-consciousness.
No-self / Foucault / Derrida
无我和主体解构不是反驳，而是提醒：自我是过程性、关系性、制度性生成物。你的理论回应：自我性不是实体，而是层级条件与调度结构。
No-self and subject-deconstruction do not refute the theory; they warn that the self is processual, relational, and institutionally produced. The response: selfhood is not a substance, but a layered condition and dispatch structure.
